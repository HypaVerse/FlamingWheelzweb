The Hyper Realistic Racing Game

Flames are the main characters in the flaming Wheelz game. They are playable NFTs which also allow holders the opportunity to own governance rights, priority access, VIP status, etc. Our NFTs come in two collections; The Genesis and the Utility mints;
Genesis Edition
The Genesis edition comes with benefits like VIP access, priority access to all new and future Hypatia products, early investors benefits, placeholders for airdrops, passive income, and some special in-game accesses.
Hypatia Token HGT
A governance meeting/voting will be held to decide whether holders of utility NFTs can burn an amount of NFTs to upgrade to Genesis or a special rank NFT to allow some benefits to being enjoyed.
Genesis Mint
The Genesis mints allow users to access VIP status in-game, priority access to all future Hypatia products, Earn passive income and also have a soft voice in our private game development channel on discord. While the Genesis mints grant all these, it comes in two versions; the pre-mints and the Genesis cards.
pre-mint cards
The pre-mint cards come with special abilities in-game, a low price, and 25% moneyback in Hypatia Token which is the main in-game currency and governance token of our ecosystem and still a placeholder for all now and future airdrops as well as all other benefits associated with the Genesis card.
Utility Edition
The utility edition allows investors who will get in later to play the game; this edition has some special benefits in-game which allow holders to participate in special and limited-time events to earn

Our Long Term
Strategy  

Treasury Investments
Looking at sustaining our ecosystem, our team have followed in the footsteps of projects with in-house finance experts who have crafted strategic investment plan for their project and have adopted this to implement in the Hypatia Games ecosystem.
Hypatia Games Token HGT utility
Adding utility for our native token will establish long-term buy pressure and thus generates positive price action. To achieve this, we are developing an entire ecosystem around $HGT, with the Calculus NFT marketplace being the first major branch of the ecosystem.
Official BuyBacks
The returns from these investments will be used to initiate buybacks for $HGT which will maintain positive price action.
